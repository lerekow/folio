About Me

I am an aspiring artist based in Connecticut. I earned my Associates in Graphic Design and Illustration at CT State Tunxis. Over the years, I filled many positions within the Tunxis Photo & Art Club and had the honor of designing its current mascot, Captain, and other promotional material. My work has been featured in the Tunxis Sun, the student literary magazine FAB, and various fan-community zines.
